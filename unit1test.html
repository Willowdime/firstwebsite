3.1 Review the IT tools used for this project

On this one. I would like to directly compare the software I used to highlight their specific benefits and drawbacks.

Although I stated in Unit 1 Outcome 2.1, that I could do this entire project in VSCode, it would have taken twice as long. Organising this project would have been much more challenging and nowhere near as readable and condensable as Trello. Though there are spellcheckers for VSCode, they pale in comparison to the near human English teacher levels of accuracy that you get from Microsoft Word. Likewise, Trello may offer some spell-checking services, but adding large blocks of text conflict with the readability of its intended purpose on such a short project. VSCode offers features similar to Word, but centred around coding. While I could write out a huge block of code in Word, I there would be no syntax-finishing, error messages, debugging, syntax highlighting or even the right kind of predictive text.
